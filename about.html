What is Women Empowerment?

Women empowerment focuses on improving the social, economic, educational, and political strength of women. It allows women to make their own decisions and contribute positively to society.

Why It Matters

  • Promotes gender equality
  • Improves education and employment
  • Strengthens families and communities

Empowering women means empowering the nation
